Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy explains how Stu Perry Therapeutic Coaching ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects any personal information you provide when using this website (stuperry.com).

What information I collect

When you use the contact form on this website, I collect your name, email address, and any information you choose to share in your message. I do not collect any other personal data automatically through this website.

How I use your information

The information you provide through the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry and to explore whether working together is a good fit. I will not use your details to send unsolicited marketing communications, and I will not share your information with any third parties except as described below.

Third party services

This website uses Formspree to process contact form submissions. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted securely to Formspree and forwarded to my email address. Please refer to Formspree's privacy policy for details of how they handle your data.

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server log data including IP addresses. Please refer to GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. No analytics software is installed on this site.

How long I keep your data

Enquiry emails are retained only for as long as necessary to handle your request. If you do not become a client, I will delete your contact details within 90 days of our last correspondence.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data I hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact me at stuartjperry@hotmail.com and I will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me at stuartjperry@hotmail.com or via WhatsApp.
